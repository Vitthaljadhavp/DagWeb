About Entrance Exams



    For getting entry into any field such as UG/PG, Medical, Engineering, Management or Law, One must possess an entrance exam conducted by any educational institute in state, National and international level. Potential of students in that particular field is evaluated through these entrance exams. These exams which are very competitive in nature, Teaches student to deal with competition they are about to face in their future. Students who crack these exams are selected in the top colleges. We provide you with detailed information about each exams in India which includes general information about the exam, Eligibility criteria, Important dates, Syllabus, Preparation tips and much more.

Some of the famous entrance exams in India are JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, CAT, MAT, CLAT etc. Through these exams, Students will get admission to the various institutes. These institutes offer various diploma, Bachelor & Master Degree courses in various specializations.

There are many Career in medical field. It is a dream of many students. This field is one of the booming career option. There are many universities/colleges which offer admission into various UG/PG medical courses through their institute level entrance exam or national level.

It is compulsory to appear in entrance exam and score good ranks to confirm a seat in a reputed college with a dream placement in near future.
