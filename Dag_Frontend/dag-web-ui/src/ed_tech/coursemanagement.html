  "Management studies enable you to take a responsible work as a manager through your managerial skills. Whether you aim to be self-employed, An entrepreneur, Head your own company, Or to work for private business, Not-for-profit organisations or Government agencies, Management gives you the tool for success!. Management is a set of ideology describing to the directing and controlling, Functions of planning, Organizing, And the application of these principles in harnessing physical, Financial, Human, And informational resources efficiently and effectively to achieve organizational goals. One can enter in the Management Stream with the help of CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT and various national level entrance examinations."
